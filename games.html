Game Recommendations

League of Legends

League of Legends is online team based arena game with over 120 unique champions competing each others for victory. It is considered the most popular online game worldwide and is known to be the face of the E-sports. Majority of champions are balanced in power. For beginners, I strongly recommend playing "Garen," a powerful champion that can take much damage with low risk.


Overwatch

Overwatch is team based FPS (first person shooting) game with 21 unique heroes fighting for territories. It is the most recent popular game that rivals against "League of Legends." The current strongest hero is "Widowmaker," using sniper rifle that allows you to one shot one kill.
